Adding Our Repo To Cydia

Step 1: Intro

First you will need and IOS that is jailbroken if your IOS is above 11.1.2 then you can not use.


Step 2: Assuming you are jailbroken

Open cydia
1. Go to sources
2. Edit
3. Add
4. paste or type this url in the inputbox
5. https://jurassiciphone.github.io
click add and cydia will do the rest.
